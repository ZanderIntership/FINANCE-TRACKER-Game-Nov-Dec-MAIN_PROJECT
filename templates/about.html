About This App

This is a simple finance tracker demo. Use Track Spend to add transactions (expenses/income). Visit NetWorth to add assets and visualize how your assets contribute to your net worth.

Note: The app currently uses dummy data and in-memory storage. Values shown on the Home dashboard are placeholders until real data is entered and persisted.

To make this production-ready, add persistent storage (SQLite/Postgres), authentication, and input validation.
